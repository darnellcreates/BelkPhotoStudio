My trip to the Belk Photo Studio

Illustrated and Animated by: Darnell Thomas

Hold down the "Scroll Down" button to view!

The Belk Photo studio is located about the Belk Pineville store located at Carolina Place Mall

On-staff employees who work with Product Image Producers in adding completed images to assortment pages

On staff designers receive images taken by R.R. Donnally, who is specifically hired by Belk to do all non-vendor Photography.

Photographers work aggressively to photograph new content as it arrives in our inventory, with multiple photography bays at their disposal

Shoots also include clothing and Soft/Hard home stages built to replicate household rooms!

Stage props are aplenty! There's literally a warehouse JUST consisting of props for all departments!

Completed images are sent over to Belk corporate for use and placed on our website and used in our print advertisements!
